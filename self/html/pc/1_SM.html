Biography

Seedhe Maut represent the next stage in the evolution of the capital's hip-hop sound. Having mastered the art of delivering razor sharp, combative and witty rhymes,
the duo are following in the trailblazing footsteps of international hip-hop collectives such as Run The Jewels, Clipse, Black Hippy, Mobb Deep, Blackstar and more.
